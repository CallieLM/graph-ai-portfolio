About the course

No-code AI tools for responsible task automation.

Generative AI for Work & Research is an 8-week live cohort course by The GRAPH Courses. It combines weekly workshops, help sessions, private forums, feedback on assessments, capstone coaching, a shareable certificate, and lifetime access to a tutorial library.

Course architecture

From AI foundations to a final showcase.

The course focuses on practical, responsible AI use: creating websites and documents, analyzing data, synthesizing research, building chatbots, automating tasks, communicating findings, and presenting a final project.

35% Workshop assignments
40% Final project
15% Online quizzes
80% Passing grade
